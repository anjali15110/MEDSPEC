FACILITIES

Emergency Department

A fully-equipped emergency department staffed with skilled medical professionals to handle critical situations and urgent care needs. Emergency Departments (EDs) are crucial components of hospitals and medical centers designed to provide immediate care to individuals with acute illnesses and injuries.

Surgical Suites

State-of-the-art operating rooms equipped with advanced technology for various surgical procedures. Surgical suites house multiple operating rooms equipped with surgical lights, tables, and advanced medical equipment.

Intensive Care Units (ICU)

Specialized units for critical care patients, equipped with advanced monitoring and life-support equipment. ICUs are equipped with advanced monitoring systems to continuously track vital signs, such as heart rate, blood pressure, respiratory rate, and oxygen saturation.
