Our Services

These services are designed to diagnose, treat, and prevent various medical conditions.

Cardilogy

Cardiology services involve a range of diagnostic tests to assess heart health. Common diagnostic procedures include ECG, echocardiograms, stress tests, and cardiac catheterization.

Emergency

Emergency services include rapid diagnostic assessments, such as imaging (X-rays, CT scans) and laboratory tests, to quickly identify and address the cause of the medical issue.

Baby Nursery

Healthcare providers in the baby nursery conduct routine medical assessments, including measurements of weight. They also perform screenings for common newborn conditions.

Dentist

Dentists use crowns to cover damaged teeth and bridges to replace missing teeth.Dentists visually inspect the teeth, gums, tongue, and other oral structures for signs of abnormalities.

CT scan

Used to evaluate the brain for conditions such as strokes, tumors, and head injuries. CT scans are used for diagnostic purposes to visualize internal organs, bones, blood vessels, and soft tissues.

Pure Water

Hospitals may have sophisticated water treatment systems, including reverse osmosis or other purification methods, to ensure a high level of water quality. Purified water may be used in several things.
